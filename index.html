About Tasks - Uniflow

Tasks - Uniflow is a native iOS task manager designed for real life. Use it as a standalone app for fast, private task management, or connect it to Notion, Apple Reminders, and Google Tasks to unify everything in one beautiful interface. The choice is yours—Tasks - Uniflow adapts to how you work.

✓ Local Tasks
🔄 Notion Sync
📋 Reminders Sync
🟢 Google Tasks Sync
☁️ iCloud Backup
🔍 Smart Filtering

Perfect for: Individuals who want a fast, clean task manager, Notion users wanting better mobile task management, Apple Reminders and Google Tasks users needing more organization, teams using multiple task systems, people who value privacy and local-first apps, and anyone who wants flexibility without complexity.

Key Features

Discover what makes Tasks - Uniflow the perfect task companion:

Standalone Excellence: Works perfectly without any integrations - fast, local, and private
Optional Notion Sync: Bidirectional sync with your Notion databases when you want it
Apple Reminders: Sync with Reminders, making tasks accessible to Siri and Apple Watch
Google Tasks: Bidirectional sync with your Google Tasks lists when you connect your Google account
Unified View: See all tasks together regardless of source with smart filtering
iCloud Sync: Automatic sync across all your iOS devices using your private iCloud account
Background Sync: Stays current automatically, even when the app is closed
Quick Actions: Long-press the app icon to quickly add a task

Your Privacy Matters

Local-First Design: All tasks are stored locally on your device - no cloud account required

You Stay in Control: Notion, Google Tasks, and iCloud connections are optional and use your own accounts

Secure Credentials: Notion tokens and Google sign-in tokens are stored safely in the iOS Keychain

No Tracking, No Ads: We don't collect analytics, usage data, or personal information, and there are no advertisements
